Join Our Research Group

I am always welcoming new students to collaborate with me on exciting research projects in machine vision and deep learning. Our group focuses on building controllable and reliable machine vision systems—developing methods to guide models to follow constraints, enhance interpretability, and quantify or reduce uncertainty in predictions.

📢 PhD Student Recruiting

We have openings for PhD students interested in:

  • 🔧 How can we guide models to produce outputs that follow constraints?
  • 🔎 How can we make models easier to interpret and explain?
  • 📊 How can we measure and reduce uncertainty in predictions?

If this fits you, please email your CV (with publication list, transcripts, and SOP) to hle40@charlotte.edu with the subject line [PhDApplicant-YourName].
