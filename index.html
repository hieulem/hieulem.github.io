Biography

I am currently an applied scientist at Amazon Robotics, North Reading, Massachusett, USA. Prior to joining Amazon, I obtained my Ph.D. in computer science from Stony Brook Univeristy working with Prof. Dimitris Samaras.

Research Interest

I am interested in computer vision especially low-level vision and image editing. I investigate how to incorporate traditional physics-based method and deep learning-based method for interpretable and physically plausible shadow image editing. Recently, I focus on the following research topics:
  • Shadow detection and removal
  • Few-shot classification and detection
  • Efficient object detection
