I am an Assistant Professor at UNC Charlotte and co-founder of the Charlotte Computer Vision Lab.
Prior to UNC Charlotte, I earned my PhD at Stony Brook University, worked at Amazon Robotics and EPFL.
My research centers on controllable and reliable machine vision, with the goal of building models that can follow constraints, provide interpretable reasoning, and make predictions with calibrated uncertainty.
